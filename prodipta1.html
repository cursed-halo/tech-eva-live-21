Beginner of Coding


- Prodipta Pal

ECE

Previously, I gathered my knowledge from the primary programming language, which is C. When our college started a VAT (Value Added Training) training on Web development, IOT and Robotics, Ethical Hacking, and AutoCAD. I chose IOT and Robotics. I was passionate about gaining my knowledge of Robotics. Firstly, it is too tough for me to understand the Arduino programming. But it is a little bit easy after helping of my friends and regarded teacher.



I want to expertise myself in other programming languages. So if you a beginner of programming, you must practice yourself on this.
